KRAS and NRAS are part of the
RAS family of oncogenes1

RAS status definitions2:

  • RAS mutation negative, or wild type (WT), means the RAS genes (KRAS and NRAS) do not have mutations
  • RAS mutation positive, or mutant type (MT), means that at least one of the RAS genes (KRAS or NRAS) contains a mutation that affects its function

mCRC = metastatic colorectal cancer; MT = mutant type; WT = wild type.
